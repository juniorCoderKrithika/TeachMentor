Quiz

This quiz is designed for teachers to gain insights into their students learning styles, aiming to create a more personalised educational experience. It explores preferences for visual, auditory, or hands-on learning, along with study environments, social interaction, and technology preferences. By understanding these aspects,teachers can adapt their methods to better suit individual student needs enhancing engagement and effectiveness in learning.
